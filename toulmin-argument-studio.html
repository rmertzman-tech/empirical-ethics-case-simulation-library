Purpose

This studio combines the Phase N Critical Thinking Bridge with a lighter extraction from the earlier Toulmin app. It does not collect names, emails, grades, or private student information. It helps students build a public argument from a public case.

Prompt governance:

The prompts and questions on this page are examples, suggestions, opportunities, or explorations. They are not automatic graded assignments. Instructors decide which prompts, if any, students should use, adapt, submit, discuss, or ignore.

Privacy:

Do not enter private personal, family, medical, legal, employment, or financial information into public tools. Public tools are for shared case analysis. Personal application belongs only in private course spaces when your instructor directs it.
