Use sequence

  1. Read the assigned EBB chapter or instructor introduction.
  2. Open the matching Capability Lab activity when your instructor uses it in class.
  3. Open the matching EECSL public case below.
  4. Use the case prompts as discussion opportunities or optional reflection starters, not as automatic graded assignments.
  5. Move private reflections into your Navigator, course notebook, or instructor-approved LMS space.
Public-site boundary:

Do not enter private personal information here. EECSL is a public teaching site. Private reflection belongs in a private tool or approved course space.
