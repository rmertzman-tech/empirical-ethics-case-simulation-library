The Full Structural Spectrum

The spectrum places SPC beside other higher-education institutions and cross-sector benchmarks. The point is not to collapse all sectors into sameness, but to ask whether a common structural ceiling appears across different institutional architectures.

Saint Petersburg College in Context

The source app treats SPC as a structural telescope: close enough to students to be meaningful, but public enough to analyze without private disclosure.

Key classroom move:

The SPC / UF comparison is not a claim that the institutions are the same. It asks how two very different public institutions can show near-equivalent structural capacity scores at very different student costs.

The Exit Option Finding

Higher-ed extraction tier

High-extraction higher education appears in the source app around FHRI_max 27–33. Students can exit, but often with debt, lost credits, or opportunity cost.

Captive-population tier

Captive or constrained sectors appear around FHRI_max 11–14 in the source app. Exit may be blocked by incarceration, cognitive impairment, foreclosure, or institutional dependency.

Formal hypothesis

The exit option modulates how low the structural ceiling can fall, not whether the system can become extractive.

Testable prediction:

If exit cost is highest within higher education — non-transferable credits, debt already incurred, locked-in employer tuition routes — FHRI_max should be lower than where exit is easier.

Sign intact

Colleges operate. Degrees are awarded. Accreditation is maintained. Students enroll and classes meet.

Signal accessed

The source app identifies the tuition revenue allocation channel and the 1992 HEA amendments expanding Title IV access for for-profit institutions.

Interpretant replaced

Original: tuition as investment in educational capacity. Replaced: enrollment as revenue unit serviced by federal loan guarantees, with risk externalized.

SPC location

SPC's lower ε suggests the replacement has not propagated in the same way as in the high-extraction for-profit comparison.
