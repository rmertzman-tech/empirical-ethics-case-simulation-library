Prompt and privacy governance

Any suggested discussion or journal prompts are examples, suggestions, opportunities, or explorations. They are not automatic graded assignments. Instructors decide what to use, adapt, grade, replace, or omit.

Students should not enter private personal information into public case tools. Private application belongs in the course notebook, private discussion space, or Navigator route when the instructor directs it.
