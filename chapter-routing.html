How to use this routing

Use these routes as teaching suggestions. Instructors may preview a case early, return to it in the listed chapter, or use it as a capstone comparison.

Boundary:

EECSL cases are public classroom materials. Private personal reflection belongs in the Navigator, a notebook, or an instructor-approved course space.
