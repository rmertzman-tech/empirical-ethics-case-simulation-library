OS1 felt response:

What seems morally obvious, troubling, unfair, dignifying, dangerous, or urgent in the public case?

Claim:

What public claim does that response suggest?

Grounds:

What evidence, simulation result, or case detail supports the claim?

Warrant:

Why do the grounds support the claim?

Backing:

What ethical framework, principle, course concept, or public standard supports the warrant?

Qualifier:

How certain is the claim? What limits or assumptions should be stated?

Rebuttal:

What would weaken, challenge, or complicate the claim?

OS2 coordination statement:

What shared repair proposal, public question, or next step could others examine even if they do not share your OS1 reaction?
